What else you have to know about poodles?

  • While other dogs have two layers of fur that grow to a certain length and then shed, poodles have a light undercoat with long strands of extremely curly hair on top.
  • Like ours, their hair can tangle and form mats easily. This is why it's so important to bath, groom, and brush your poodle regularly.
